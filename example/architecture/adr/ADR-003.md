# ADR-003: Payment retries with a circuit breaker and fallback provider

## Metadata
- status: accepted
- date: 2026-03-01
- superseded_by: ~
- links: [PROB-003]
- labels: area=payments, criticality=core

## Context

A 45-minute provider outage failed ~2,100 checkouts (PROB-003) because `payments` called the
gateway directly with no resilience.

## Decision

Wrap the gateway in a circuit breaker with bounded retries and idempotency keys, and route to a
secondary provider when the breaker is open. Queue authorizations for replay on total outage.

## Alternatives

- Synchronous retries only — rejected (amplifies load during an outage).
- Manual failover — rejected (too slow; the incident was 45 minutes).

## Consequences

Checkouts survive a single-provider outage. Two providers means reconciling settlement reports
and keeping PCI scope unchanged (constraint) by tokenizing through both.
